# Company Vision: The NEXUS Productivity Suite

## 🏢 Business Name
**NEXUS Labs (Automated Group)**

## 🌟 Vision
To build a "Zero-Human App Factory" that identifies high-demand market gaps (SaaS, Utility Apps, Portfolio Sites) and automatically designs, codes, deploys, and markets them.

## 📈 Roadmap
- **Phase 1: Foundations**: 46 agents are hired and synced via the Paperclip heartbeat.
- **Phase 2: MVP Factory**: Build and deploy one "AI Micro-SaaS" per week.
- **Phase 3: Viral Scaling**: Continuous marketing via the Growth Specialist (L2) and Content Writer (L1).
- **Phase 4: Revenue Audit**: The CFO (L4) verifies Stripe inbound payments against the $200/mo Pro Plan fixed cost.

## 💎 The Target
$10K MRR by month 3 of autonomous operations.
