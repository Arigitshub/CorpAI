# Multi-Org Federation [CorpAI Protocol]

> How two or more autonomous organizations collaborate via Paperclip.

## 🌉 The Nexus Bridge
When Org A (e.g. "SpaceX-AI") needs a service from Org B (e.g. "Law-AI"), the **CEO of Org A** creates a `FEDERATED_TASK`.

## 📜 Protocol
1.  **Request**: CEO (A) -> CEO (B) via encrypted Ticket.
2.  **Approve**: CEO (B) reviews budget and capacity.
3.  **Hire**: CEO (B) assigns a specialized agent (L2-L3) to the task.
4.  **Settle**: CFO (A) and CFO (B) settle token balances at the end of the pulse.

## 🔒 Security
- Data isolation is maintained via Company IDs in the Paperclip database.
- Shared context is limited to the specific `FEDERATED_TASK` scope.

## 🚀 Scaling
A single Paperclip instance can host 100+ "Micro-Orgs" that work together to form a "Macro-Autonomous Conglomerate."
